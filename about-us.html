ABOUT US

Aadgad Remedies is a fast-growing pharmaceutical distribution and marketing company situated in Jaunpur, Uttar Pradesh, dedicated to easing the conveyance of medicines between manufacturers and healthcare providers in India. Mr. Tej Bahadur Singh, the founder of Aadgad Remedies, has been an active practitioner in the field of medicine distribution since 2014.

We, at Aadgad Remedies are a purposive and determined team of more than 10 people engaged in the distribution and promotion of a wide range of medicinal products significantly effective towards bacterial infections, infections, allergies, and much more. We offer our customers supreme quality pharma products with over 20 medicines to help cure illnesses and injuries. For more information, refer to our range of products.

With our pharmaceutical marketing services, we are on a mission to connect pharmaceutical manufacturers, wholesalers, healthcare providers, and patients, as one. In the past 7 years, we have supported several pharma companies by boosting their online and offline presence through our wide range of marketing strategies and services. Likewise, we are confident that we can help you too, bring your business to the next level.

Mission

To fulfill our societal obligations of bringing high-quality healthcare to the reach of all, while remaining true to our fundamental principles of honesty, ethics, and dedication; and through excellence in technology, innovative methodologies, and customer satisfaction, attain leadership in select markets, products, and services across the world.

Vision

Our vision is to become India's top pharmaceutical distributor, and a key worldwide player by offering high-quality, secure, cost-effective, timely, medical and therapeutic deliveries. We aim to be known as a proactive contributor to the pharmaceutical industry, that is driven by high standards and creative ambitions. We aspire to create a difference in society by developing a portfolio of low-cost medications that can reach every nook and corner of the world.
